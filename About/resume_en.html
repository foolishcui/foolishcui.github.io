Self Introduction

My name is Zhitong Cui, and I majored in computer science in Beijing University of Technology. Through courses in college I learned the basic operating principle of computer and how to develop a website, which helps me understand design from a deeper level. And now I'm a graduate student in ZheJiang University studying interaction design.

Talking about design, everything for me starts from an amazing computer called 'Macbook'. It's beautiful industrial design and friendly user experience bring me passion to create something like this. So I began to learn interaction design on my own and took up my first internship. During my internship We developed an app called 'Have Fun Sports based on 'WeChat', which can remind people watching sports lives they care about. In the end we have four hundred users on our campus. My design and front-end development ability were improved a lot during this time.

And then I put myself to work as an UX design intern in App Annie, a company analyze apps' market data. I help to build a component library for product and unify user experience for product. And during this time I communicate a lot with many foreign designers which greatly improves my oral English.

Right now I'm working on my side project, aiming to develop a mini program on WeChat to help people give opinions on their interested movies.

In thde meantime, I also did some part-time design jobs, which strengthens my design skills and help me accumulate rich experience in working with PMs and developers.

As for hobbies, I love motorcycle because every parts of it is carefully calculated and designed, I can learn a lot of mechanical knowledge from it. Also I love movies, I dream someday that I can work in a crew to witness how a real movie is shooted.
