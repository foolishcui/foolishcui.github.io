Self Introduction

My name is Cuizhitong, and I major in computer science in Beijing University of Technology. Through courses in college I learned the basic operating principle of computer and how to develop a website, which helps me understand design from a deeper level.

Talking about design, everything for me starts from an amazing computer called 'Macbook'. It's beautiful industrial design and friendly user experience bring me passion to create something like this. So I began to study interaction design on my own and took up an internship. We have developed an app called 'Have Fun Sports based on 'WeChat', which can remind people to watch sports lives they care about. We have four hundred users on our campus. My design and front-end development ability were improved a lot during this time.

I always enjoy the process of designing to provide the simplest solution for complex problems. As a result, I want to get more professional knowledge and explore more design areas. Zhejiang University gives me a perfect chance. It has great teachers as well as advanced laboratories. Also, HangZhou is an attractive city for its nice people, city culture, and beautiful sceneries (except for the buses).

After the examination, I put myself to work as an UX design intern in App Annie, a company analyze apps' market data. I help to build a component library and define consistent user experience for product. And I work with many foreign designers which improves my oral English a lot.

Besides, I'm working on my graduation project aiming to develop an 3D augmented reality game related to motorcycles, the interaction design will be easy for children to assembly different parts to their own motorcycle. The game can be realized by Vuforia AR SDK based on Unity platform using JavaScript.

As for hobbies, I love riding by motorcycle because every parts of it is carefully calculated and designed, I can learn a lot of mechanical knowledge from it. It's great chance for me to feel the company of family or friends, feel the nature, feel the wind beside you and feel freedom.
